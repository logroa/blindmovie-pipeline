This is a daily movie game where you hear a soundbyte from a movie and try to guess which movie it's from. The goal is to correctly guess the movie in the fewest amount of guesses, but if you guess wrong after hearing a clip, we'll give you another one that should make it easier. There are five possible clips, so if you get it wrong for all five, you lose that day's game.
